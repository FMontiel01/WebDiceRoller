Monopoly Dice Roller

Roll two dice to determine how many spaces to move. The Roll Dice button is focused — press Enter to roll again.

Instructions

  1. This tool rolls two 6-sided dice for Monopoly (values 1–6).
  2. The dice roll automatically when the page loads.
  3. Press Enter or click Roll Dice to roll again.
  4. If you roll a double, your doubles streak increases.
  5. On 3 doubles in a row, the app indicates “Go to Jail”.
  6. Click Reset Turn to clear the doubles streak (start a new turn).
